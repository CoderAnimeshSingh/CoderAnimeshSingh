# Viva Voce Preparation — SmartSpend AI

## Q1. What is the main objective of your project?
To help users manage personal finances through structured tracking, budgeting, and actionable insights.

## Q2. Which real-world problem does it solve?
It solves irregular expense tracking and overspending due to lack of visibility and discipline.

## Q3. Why did you select this project?
It is practical, relevant to a broad audience, and technically rich enough for a major project.

## Q4. Why this technology stack?
Node + Express enabled rapid backend development, SQLite simplified deployment, and vanilla JS kept frontend lightweight.

## Q5. Explain your database design.
The database is normalized around users, categories, transactions, and budgets to reduce redundancy and maintain integrity.

## Q6. How do you secure user data?
Passwords are hashed using bcrypt; authenticated routes require JWT tokens.

## Q7. How is responsiveness achieved?
CSS grid and flexible layout techniques are used for mobile and desktop compatibility.

## Q8. What is your innovation point?
Monthly budget status is translated into insight messages that guide users toward financial discipline.

## Q9. Can this project be launched commercially?
Yes, through a freemium SaaS model with premium analytics and budget advisory features.

## Q10. What improvements will you add later?
Automatic receipt scanning, predictive expense forecasting, and regional language support.
